Privacy Policy

Data Controller: Anurag Kanakapalli
Website: kanakapalli.in

Contact Information

Users may contact the Data Controller through the contact form on the website or by using the appointment scheduling system provided by Calendly, for any inquiries related to personal data management.

Types of Data Collected

The site may collect the following types of data:

Data Voluntarily Provided by the User: Name, surname, email address, and telephone number (through contact forms or Calendly).

Data Automatically Collected: IP address, browsing data, user preferences, and anonymous data for statistical analysis.

Purposes and Legal Basis of Processing

Purposes: Respond to contact requests.

  • Schedule appointments (via Calendly).
  • Improve the user’s browsing experience on the website.
  • Perform anonymous traffic analysis.
  • Carry out marketing activities (with the user’s prior consent, such as sending newsletters).

Legal Basis: The legal basis for processing includes user consent, the execution of a contract, or the legitimate interest of the Data Controller.

Tools and Services Used

The website utilizes the following tools to manage data:

  • Webflow: Hosting and website management.
  • Google Analytics: Analysis of user traffic and behavior (anonymized where possible).
  • Calendly: Appointment scheduling.
  • Finsweet Cookie Banner: Management of cookie consent.
  • Other Marketing or Analytics Tools: Such as Facebook Pixel or Google Ads, if applicable.

Data Sharing

Personal data may be shared with:

  • Hosting service providers (e.g., Webflow).
  • Analytics service providers (e.g., Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel).
  • Other third-party partners for technical and marketing purposes.

Data will never be sold or transferred for purposes not stated in this Privacy Policy.

Security Measures

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including:

  • Encryption.
  • Restricted access to data.
  • Secure backup systems.

Data Retention Period

  • Contact Data: Retained for a maximum of 120 days.
  • Anonymous Data: Retained indefinitely for statistical analysis.

User Rights

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Access: Obtain a copy of personal data.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request the deletion of personal data.
  • Restriction: Limit the processing of personal data.
  • Portability: Receive personal data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Revoke previously given consent.

To exercise these rights, users may use the contact form on the website or schedule an appointment through Calendly.

International Data Transfers

If personal data are transferred outside the European Union, appropriate safeguards will be implemented to ensure adequate protection of user rights.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Users will be notified of any changes via a notice on the website.

For more information, users may contact the Data Controller by using the contact form or the Calendly appointment system.
