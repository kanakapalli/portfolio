Overview

Archymos (a fictitious name used to protect the clients privacy), a renowned company in the chemical industry specializing in the production of resins and other synthetic materials, sought to revamp its website to better reflect its brand identity and enhance user engagement.The project was commissioned by Netfolie, with Founder and President Dennis Markessinis and his team providing essential resources, including the brand book, site structure, content documents, and brand assets.

View the
figma project

process

Step 1
Setting Up the Working Environment

To ensure an organized and consistent design process, I began by preparing the working environment using the Lumos design libraries. On the right, you can see a summary board showcasing all the variables that were set.

This approach allowed me to:

Establish Project Variables

Defined colors, typography styles (H1, H2, H3, etc.), spacing etc...

Streamline Workflow

Facilitated efficient updates and adjustments throughout the project.

Maintain Consistency

Ensured that all design elements adhered to Archymos' brand guidelines.

Step 2
Drafting the Homepage

With the foundational elements in place, I proceeded to design an initial draft of the homepage. The final design of the homepage is displayed on the right.

Client Collaboration

Sent the draft to Netfolie for approval.

Feedback Implementation

Incorporated minor adjustments based on the client's input.

Finalization

Achieved a design that met Archymos' vision and brand identity.

Step 3
Designing the
Remaining Pages

Building on the approved homepage design, I moved forward with the other pages. You can view the complete project overview here.

Placeholder Text Usage

Since some content was incomplete, I used lorem ipsum text where necessary, as requested by Netfolie.

Consistent Design Language

Applied the established design variables to ensure a cohesive look and feel across all pages.

Preparation for Content Integration

Structured the layouts to accommodate future content seamlessly.

Outcome

The project culminated in a cohesive and visually engaging website design that:

Aligns with Archymos' Brand

Reflects the company's identity and values.

Enhances User Experience

Provides intuitive navigation and clear information hierarchy.

Facilitates Future Updates

Allows for easy integration of final content without altering the design structure.

Key
Takeaways

Effective Use of Design Libraries

Leveraging Lumos ensured consistency and efficiency.

Strong Client Communication

Regular updates and feedback loops with Netfolie led to timely approvals.

Adaptability

Utilizing placeholder text kept the project on schedule despite incomplete content.

Conclusion

This project showcases the importance of a structured approach and effective collaboration in web design. By integrating design and development processes and maintaining open communication with Netfolie, we delivered a website that not only meets Archymos' current needs but is also adaptable for future growth.
